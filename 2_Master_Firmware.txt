STEP 2: FIRMWARE UPDATE (NI-SERIES)
===============================================================================
MANDATORY: Your NI-Master must be running a modern firmware version to 
support current IP communication and NetLinx Studio 4 features.

REQUIRED FILE: 2_Firmware.kit (Included in this directory)

---
INSTRUCTIONS FOR UPDATING VIA NETLINX STUDIO 4:
---

1. CONNECT TO THE MASTER:
   - Use the "Program" port (Serial) or a known IP address.
   - In NetLinx Studio, go to: Settings > Workspace Communications Settings.
   - Verify that 'Online Tree' shows your Master (Device 0).

2. OPEN FIRMWARE TRANSFER:
   - Go to: Tools > Firmware Transfer > Send to NetLinx Device.

3. SELECT THE .KIT FILE:
   - Click 'Add' and navigate to the "2_Firmware.kit" file in this repo.
   - Ensure the Target is set to: System 1, Device 0 (The Master).

4. EXECUTE THE TRANSFER:
   - Click 'Send'. 
   - CRITICAL: Do NOT power off the unit during this process. 
   - The Master will reboot automatically. This may take 3-5 minutes.

5. VERIFICATION:
   - Once the "Online" LED stays solid, open the Terminal (NSH).
   - Type: 'show platform' or 'ver'.
   - Confirm the version matches the .kit file details.

If the Master reboots and you can see it in the Online Tree, move to 
file: 3_Device_Software.txt.
===============================================================================
