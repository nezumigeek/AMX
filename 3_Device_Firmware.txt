STEP 3: DEVICE FIRMWARE (SUB-DEVICES & CARDS)
===============================================================================
MANDATORY: Updating the Master (.kit) is only half the battle. You must also 
ensure the internal devices and physical expansion cards are updated.

REQUIRED FILES: 
- Look for .kit files specifically labeled for "Internal Devices" or 
  specific cards (e.g., NXC-COM2, NXC-REL8).

---
INSTRUCTIONS:
---

1. IDENTIFY SUB-DEVICES:
   - In NetLinx Studio, look at your 'Online Tree'.
   - You will see Device 0 (Master), but also Device 5001 (Onboard Ports).
   - If you have an NI-4100 with cards, you will see 5002, 5003, etc.

2. THE "DEVICE" TRANSFER:
   - Go to: Tools > Firmware Transfer > Send to NetLinx Device.
   - Click 'Add' and select your Device .kit file.
   - TARGETING IS DIFFERENT HERE: 
     Instead of targeting Device 0, you must target the specific Device 
     number shown in your Online Tree (usually 5001 for onboard I/O).

3. WHY THIS MATTERS:
   - Without this, the Master might be running v4.x firmware while the 
     serial ports are still trying to communicate using code from 2008. 
     This leads to "Device Offline" toggling and buffer overruns.

Once the "Output" window in Studio confirms "Transfer Complete" for 
each device, proceed to: 4_Device_Mapping.txt.
